About us


We are an Austrian company based in Vienna, producing natural, antibiotic-free feed additives since 2009. We serve all major livestock species like cattle, pigs, poultry, horses, and more. Certified with QS and GMP+, we offer safe, effective, and practical solutions for modern farming. Today, our products are used in over 30 countries, helping raise healthier animals with less dependence on antibiotics.

What Challenges We Help Solve

Livestock producers face growing pressure to reduce antibiotic use, improve animal health naturally, and control production costs — all while meeting strict market and regulatory demands.


To face these challenges, we’ve developed unique products that help you achieve:


• Antibiotic-free prevention of bacterial and viral diseases

• Stronger immunity and natural resistance

• Lower mortality rates across livestock

• Reduced veterinary treatment costs

• Better feed efficiency and reduced consumption

• Faster growth and improved weight gain

• Sustainable long-term animal health through natural support

Our Key Innovations

VF Care

Targeted support for immunity and udder health.


VF Care is a natural feed additive formulated to strengthen the immune system, support recovery, and help reduce antibiotic use. It is especially effective in the prevention and supportive treatment of mastitis, promoting udder health, reducing inflammation, and improving milk yield. With its plant-based composition, VF Care also enhances feed intake, boosts resilience during stress, and supports overall productivity.

Ecopol

Reduces Broiler Growth Time by 2 Days


ECOPOL is a natural liquid additive that improves digestion, enhances nutrient absorption, and boosts immunity. It helps animals cope with stress, increases body weight, and supports intestinal health. With proven antioxidant, antiviral, and antimicrobial properties, ECOPOL reduces mortality, strengthens the immune system, and improves overall performance — all without antibiotics.
